Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom branded bags and packaging services

What is the minimum order quantity for custom branded bags? +

Our minimum order is 500 bags for custom printed packaging. This allows us to provide competitive pricing while ensuring premium print quality. For larger orders (5,000+ bags), we offer additional bulk discounts.

How long does it take to produce custom branded bags? +

Standard production time is 2-3 weeks after design approval. We also offer rush production (7-10 days) for urgent orders. Shipping time across Canada is typically 3-5 business days depending on location.

Can you match my brand colors exactly (Pantone)? +

Yes! We offer Pantone color matching for all custom printed bags. Our design team will work with you to ensure your brand colors are perfectly matched. We provide digital proofs and physical samples before production.

Are your paper bags really eco-friendly and recyclable? +

Absolutely! All our paper bags are made from FSC-certified paper, are 100% recyclable and biodegradable. Our kraft paper bags are unbleached and use water-based inks for printing, making them completely eco-friendly.

Do you ship to all provinces in Canada? +

Yes! We ship coast-to-coast across Canada including Ontario, Quebec, British Columbia, Alberta, and all other provinces and territories. We offer competitive shipping rates with tracking on all orders.

Can I see a sample before placing a large order? +

Yes! We provide physical samples for a small fee (which is credited back on your first order). This allows you to feel the quality and see the exact colors before committing to production.
