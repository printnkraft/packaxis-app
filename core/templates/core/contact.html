Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our packaging services

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order is typically 500 bags for custom printed orders. For stock items, we have no minimum quantity.

How long does production take?

Standard production is 2-3 weeks. Rush orders can be completed in 7-10 business days for an additional fee.

Do you ship across Canada?

Yes! We ship to all provinces and territories. Orders over $2,000 qualify for free shipping anywhere in Canada.

Can I get a sample before ordering?

Absolutely! We provide free samples of our stock products. Custom printed samples are available for a small fee.
